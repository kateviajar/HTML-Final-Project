About LetsBuy

LetsBuy is the global marketplace for unique and creative goods. It’s home to a universe of special, extraordinary items of vintage treasures.

Our platform connects them with millions of buyers looking for an alternative—something special with a human touch, for those moments in life that deserve imagination.

As a company, we strive to lead with our guiding principles and to help spread ideas of sustainability and responsibility whose impact can reach far beyond our own business.
